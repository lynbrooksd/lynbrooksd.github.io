FAQs

You’ve Got Questions. We’ve Got Answers.




I have speech and debate experience. Should I still attend?

Yes! We have a more advanced curriculum for experienced and returning students. Our camp will be split into labs based on years of experience and difficulty level.



I have no speech and debate experience. Is this camp for me?

Yes! Directed towards 4th to 9th graders this camp is a great starting point as students will be learning both public speaking and debate.



How should I register two students?

Fill out the registration form separately for each student and make 1 payment. Be sure to mention both of their names while paying.



I have more questions. How can I get in touch?

If you have more questions please feel free to email us at admins@lynbrooksd.org
