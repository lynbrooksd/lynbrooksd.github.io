Payments


Please pay the required amount for your tournament/event. Check the event on tournament's tabroom/joy of tournaments page to find out the required amount. In the PayPal link, when you are paying, please put your child's NAME, GRADE, and the EVENT(S) AND TOURNAMENT they are signing up for. When prompted, choose the "Send Money to Family and Friends" option. If the payments do not follow these requirements, you may be required to pay again.
